Why this club is different

Where mystery meets mastery.

True Crime Toastmasters is a specialty club for people who love great stories, sharp thinking, dramatic delivery, and the thrill of a good unanswered question.

We practice the same core Toastmasters skills — speaking, listening, leadership, evaluation, and impromptu thinking — but with a twist: mystery themes, detective-style prompts, creative table topics, and meetings that feel alive from the first clue.

The guest experience

No interrogation required.

You can watch, participate, laugh, solve, speculate, or simply enjoy a meeting that proves public speaking does not have to feel stiff, scary, or beige.

Last case file: Unsolved Mysteries.

Our June 4 meeting brought together two prepared speeches, creative table topics, and a room full of mystery energy.

  • Leo Smith delivered “Caught on Camera, Still Missing,” a documentary-style speech about unresolved cases and the limits of surveillance.
  • Hassan presented on Yaya’s Reentry Program and the human challenge of rebuilding life after incarceration.
  • Table Topics mixed True Confessions with Tall Tales, sending Stonehenge, Amelia Earhart, UFOs, and the Bermuda Triangle through the Toastmasters evidence lab.
  • The verdict: serious storytelling, useful feedback, and just enough weird to make the meeting memorable.
