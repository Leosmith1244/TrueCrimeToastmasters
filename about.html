About the Club

The Origin Story

True Crime Toastmasters is a brand-new specialty club founded by Jennifer in Saint Paul, Minnesota. Born from a dual passion for compelling storytelling and criminal justice, this club offers a unique twist on the traditional Toastmasters experience.


Instead of standard speeches, we deal in cold cases, forensic analysis, and closing arguments. Whether you are discussing a famous heist or the psychology of a con artist, this is the safe space to unleash your inner detective.
