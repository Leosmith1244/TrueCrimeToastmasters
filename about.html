About True Crime Toastmasters

True Crime Toastmasters is a new hybrid club in Minneapolis built for people who love learning, asking smart questions, and telling compelling stories—while developing real communication and leadership skills.

What you’ll experience

  • Supportive speaking practice—prepared speeches and impromptu speaking
  • Encouraging evaluations focused on growth
  • Leadership roles that build confidence and presence
  • Optional “detective” themed prompts: clarity, logic, and persuasion

Our purpose

Toastmasters International is a global organization that helps people become more effective communicators and leaders. We reflect the Toastmasters values of Integrity, Respect, Service, and Excellence.

We also support Toastmasters Pathways—a structured learning program that helps members build skills step-by-step.

Where Leaders Are Made isn’t a slogan for us—it’s the meeting-by-meeting outcome.
