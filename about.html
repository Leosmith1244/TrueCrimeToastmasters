About the club

Not just another meeting. A memorable entry point into Toastmasters.

True Crime Toastmasters is a specialty club designed for people who are fascinated by stories, patterns, motives, evidence, psychology, and powerful communication.

We use that natural curiosity to help members become stronger speakers and leaders. The result is a club that feels smart, current, and conversation-worthy while staying grounded in Toastmasters education.

What we value
  • Supportive learning and respectful feedback
  • Curiosity, clarity, and compelling communication
  • Leadership growth through meeting roles and service
  • Toastmasters values: Integrity, Respect, Service, and Excellence
Curiosity

A theme people instantly understand

The club catches attention because it connects with a real interest many adults already have.

Growth

A stronger reason to keep coming back

Members improve through prepared speaking, impromptu speaking, evaluations, and leadership roles.

Community

A room full of like-minded people

Shared interest creates instant connection, which helps new members feel like they belong faster.
